## Important: respecting copyright of sources

ASKM offers independent analysis to help people find and understand accessibility sources.

ASKM encourages its users to consult the original sources. When an accessibility source is copyright protected, ASKM should summarize or comment on it, not replace or reproduce it.

### What ASKM may include

* Bibliographic metadata such as document titles, editions, publication dates, and publisher names
* Identifiers for purposes of citation, such as clause numbers and section titles
* Summaries and explanations written by ASKM contributors
* Brief quotations, with clear attribution, for purposes of comment and review

### What ASKM should not include

ASKM should not include substantial portions of copyright-protected sources.
